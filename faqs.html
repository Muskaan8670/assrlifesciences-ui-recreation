01. What products and medicines does ASSR Life Sciences offer?

We supply a broad portfolio of allopathic medicines, tablets, capsules, syrups, injectables, and custom formulations, under our own brand as well as OEM supply.

02. Are your medicines manufactured under certified standards?

Yes, we adhere to GMP, regulatory compliance, and quality assurance protocols to ensure the safety and efficacy of every product.

03. Do you accept bulk or institutional orders (hospitals, clinics, pharmacies)?

Absolutely. We welcome bulk orders and institutional partnerships, with special pricing and logistics support for healthcare setups.

04. Can I get custom formulations or private-label production?

Yes, we offer private-label / contract manufacturing, subject to regulatory approvals and product feasibility.

01. How do I place an order, and what is the process?

You can contact us via email or phone (listed on our Contact page). Our team will guide you through the product catalogue, quotation, order confirmation, and delivery.

02. What is your delivery timeline and logistics coverage?

We operate PAN-India distribution. Typical processing & dispatch time is within 24–48 hours after confirmation (depending on stock & location).

03. How do you ensure regulatory compliance and documentation?

Our operations include handling drug licenses, invoices, quality control reports, and all required documentation to maintain legal compliance.

04. What are your payment terms and modes?

We support multiple payment methods, including bank transfer, UPI, and credit facilities for established partners (subject to approval).

01. Can small pharmacies or new distributors partner with ASSR?

Yes, we encourage partnerships of various scales. You may apply via our Contact page, and we’ll review your business credentials.

02. What makes ASSR Life Sciences different from other pharma suppliers?

We combine affordability with rigorous quality standards, fast logistics, transparent operations, and dedicated customer support.

03. If a product is out of stock, can I pre-book or waitlist?

Yes, for certain high-demand items, we allow advance booking. Our team will notify you of expected restock dates.

04. How do you handle product returns, damages, or quality complaints?

We have a complaint resolution system. If a product is damaged or has a valid quality issue, contact us immediately for returns or replacement under warranty terms.
